Our Business Model

CareRing is built on a foundation of sustainable innovation, merging breakthrough technology with affordability to redefine elderly care. Our business model delivers exceptional value to our customers while forging lasting partnerships within the healthcare industry.

Revenue Streams

  • Device Sales: One-time purchase of the smart ring, accessible for families and healthcare institutions.
  • Subscription Services: Premium offerings—including advanced health analytics, secure cloud data storage, and AI-driven insights—available on a monthly or annual basis.
  • B2B Partnerships: Strategic collaborations with healthcare providers, insurance companies, and government programs through bulk orders and licensing agreements.

Pricing Strategy

We strike the perfect balance between cutting-edge technology and affordability, ensuring that CareRing remains accessible while delivering robust features and reliable performance.

Long-Term Vision

Our ambition is to evolve CareRing into a comprehensive AI-powered healthcare ecosystem. By expanding our services and forging strategic industry partnerships, we aim to transform preventive care and empower a healthier future for seniors worldwide.
